Welcome to the typing game "Type-Type-Mania"

How to play?

To play the game Type-Type-Mania here are the steps:


Navigate and press the "Play Game" button.

You will be then in the game menu of Type-Type-Mania.

In the Type-Type-Mania Play Game menu write in your player name which can consist maximum of 10 letters. In case you will leave the field blank, you will be notified that you will play the game as a "Anonymous" player.

Under "Select your difficulty" please select your preferred difficulty. The game has in total three difficulties. To know more about difficulties scroll down to difficulties section

You can change the volume before starting the game in case you wish to play the game blasting full volume or silently.

After setting your name and choosing your difficulty you can press on "Start" to begin with the game.

As a extra reminder the game reminds the player about the game functioning automatically on every user input key press. If the user clicked off the page after pressing "Start"button, then the user should click back on the page so it would register the keys. 

After pressing "Start" user will be navigated to the main game screen. User will see his chosen difficulty, his volume bar and is reminded where the words will appear.

The game start immediately after the countdown.

After the timer counts down and notifies to "Type!"

The user will still see his difficulty, is allowed to quit and is notified about how much time player has left on the right side. Player points are counted on the left side.

The given word that the user needs to type is located in the same place as it was notified in previous step. 

For every right letter within the word will turn from violet to white.

In case of a wrong letter the color will not change within the word.

If the player types out without a single mistake six words in a row then the player score will be multiplied by two.

If the player types out without a single mistake 15 words in a row then the player score will be multiplied by three.


If the game time is up or the player decides to end the game or loses the game then, the player will be transferred to game Scoreboard where the player will see the result of the game

DIFFICULTIES

In total there are three difficulties which have unique backgrounds, songs and penalties.

For every completed word the player gets rewarded with points.

Word length represents points and every difficulty has it own reward for each correct letter within a word. 

Player can play freely without worrying of making a mistake of losing points. 

Every single letter within a word gives you one point.

User has to be cautious as every wrong letter means -5 points from the total score . 

Every single letter within a word gives you two points.

Player can not make more than three mistakes within the game, otherwise the game will end.

User has to be cautious as every wrong letter means -10 points from the total score.

Every single letter within a word gives you three  points.
