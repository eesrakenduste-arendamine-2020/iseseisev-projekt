Welcome to the typing game "Type-Type-Mania"

How to play?

To play the game Type-Type-Mania here are the steps:


Navigate and press the "Play Game" button.

You will be then in the game menu of Type-Type-Mania.

In the game menu type in your name (Max 10 characters).

Under "Select your difficulty" please select your preferred difficulty. To know more about difficulties scroll down to difficulties section.

You can adjust your volume before starting the game.

After setting your name and choosing your difficulty you can press on "Start" to begin with the game.

After pressing "Start"you will have 5 seconds to get ready to play.

The game start immediately after the countdown.

After the timer counts down and notifies to "Type!"

Your difficulty will be displayed in the middle, score to the left and time to the right. In case you want to quit then press "Quit Game".

The word you will need to type out will be in the orange box.

Every right letter will light up white.

In case of a wrong letter the color will not change.

If you'll type out all 6 words in a row without a mistake, your next points will double.

If you'll type out all 15 words in a row without a mistake, your next points will triple.


After the game end,quitting or loss you will be moved to the Scoreboard with your score displayed below

DIFFICULTIES

In total there are three difficulties which have unique backgrounds, songs and penalties.

For every completed word the player gets rewarded with points.

Word length represents points and every difficulty has it own reward for each correct letter within a word. 

Player can play freely without worrying of making a mistake of losing points. 

Every single letter within a word gives you one point.

Game total duration is 62 seconds

User has to be cautious as every wrong letter means -5 points from the total score . 

Every single letter within a word gives you two points.

Game total duration is 63 seconds

Player can not make more than three mistakes within the game, otherwise the game will end.

User has to be cautious as every wrong letter means -10 points from the total score.

Every single letter within a word gives you three  points.

Game total duration is 54 seconds
