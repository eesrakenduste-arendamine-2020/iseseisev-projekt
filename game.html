Step by Step How 2 Play

Enter a name and pick a difficulty, then press start button and your game will start.

Type the words that appear on the screen.


In the bottom section of the game you see the scoreboard

Under each specific difficutly you will see the given difficulty scoreboard


On easy difficulty every correct word is 1 point and there are no pelanties or point reductions. Game time is 62 seconds.

On normal difficulty every correct word is 2 points, wrong letter is -5 points, no penalties. Game times is 63 seconds.

On hard difficulty every correct word is 3 points, wrong letter is -10 points, with 3 wrong letters the game will end. Game time is 54 seconds.
